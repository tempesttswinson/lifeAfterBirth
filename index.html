Welcome to Our Life After Birth!

This is our story. I’m 27 and the mother of a handsome baby boy, LJ and I’m the girlfriend of an amazing man by the name of JT. And this is Our Life After Birth.

After the birth of LJ, I suffered from postpartum depression and that has positioned me to help others that may be going through similar situations or to help a loved one who may not understand postpartum depression and how it may be affecting the one they love. In such a short period of time, I have found out how hard motherhood really is. Not that I ever thought it was easy; it’s just that no one really talks about all the hard things. Especially when you’re a new mom. When people talk about motherhood, it’s normally about the supermom that has it all together. She takes yoga classes, bakes all the goodies for the bake sale, leader of the PTA, participates in the car pool, enrolled in grad school and making all As, does arts and crafts, and teaches Sunday School, and…. and the list goes on! But what about that mom that is clueless about…EVERYTHING! Well hello that’s me. I’m here to talk about the God honest truth. I’m really here for me to have a place to let it all out and to provide that safe space for others. I’m here for the mom that just doesn’t quite undersatnd how to make ends meet or how to fit it all in one day, I’m here for the real supermoms.
